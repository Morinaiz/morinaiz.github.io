Doors & Switches

NOKIA 3310 Game Jam 3

Project Overview

Doors & Switches is my debut project, crafted during my first game jam participation. This 3D puzzle game centers around the collection and placement of spheres to manipulate doors within a Low Poly 3D Pixel-Art environment.

While many participants focused on 2D, I embraced 3D to stand out, offering a different perspective and experience within the game's thematic and technical limitations.

The Game

Leveraging my skills in Unreal Engine game development, I opted to focus on creating a 3D puzzle game. The game, themed around "ON/OFF," integrates this concept into its gameplay. Players navigate through a series of puzzles, using spheres to turn doors on (open them) or off (close them).

Development Insights

Building under the time constraints of a one-week game jam, my focus was on delivering a solid, playable puzzle experience. While initial plans included adding more complex mechanics like moving platforms and enemies, the scope was refined to ensure completion within the deadline. I ensured the game adhered to the specific rules of the jam, including palette restrictions, resolution, engine compatibility, sound design, and control schemes.

Technical Achievements

  • Created the NOKIA 3310 Template for Unreal Engine 4, publicly available on my Github.
  • Implemented shaders for Outline Detection, Pixelation, and Tonemapping to capture a distinctive visual style.
  • Configured Sound Concurrency settings to replicate the classic monophonic audio reminiscent of the NOKIA 3310 era.
  • Developed a custom movement scheme for the First-Person Character, enhancing the game's aesthetic
