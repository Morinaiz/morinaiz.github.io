Weather Effects

Upwork Project

Project Overview

While working on a 2D cartoon-style game, I developed three distinct atmospheric effects that simulate dynamic weather patterns and add a touch of realism to gameplay. These effects include a multifaceted rain-thunderstorm scenario, a dense snow-blizzard condition, and a fog ambience.
A control system for the effects was also done, which interpolates smoothly between different atmospheric conditions.

Dynamic Rain and Thunderstorm

For the rain effect, I created a system where the length of each rain particle varies with its falling speed, achieving a nice look and feel for different intensities of a downpour. The thunderstorm is brought to life with custom-made procedural lightnings, made with Unity's particle system using a ribbon. A custom shader and material work together with a global volume for color tuning, ensuring the storm's visual tone is in perfect sync with the game's art style. To complete the effect, a full-screen flash mirrors the dazzling effect of a real lightning strike.

Snow Blizzard with Mist

The snow effect is similar to its rainy counterpart. It incorporates a custom mist effect that resembles fog, creating the illusion of a snowy whiteout, with a particle effect for the snowflakes. The blending of snow particles and foggy mist conveys the blustery conditions of a blizzard, while maintaining the game's cartoon aesthetics.

Fog

A tightly optimized full-screen shader achieves the fog effect, overlaying the game with a soft mist. The fog's density and movement are controlled to mimic the flow of real fog, adding a layer of depth to the game's atmosphere.

Technical Achievements

  • Custom Weather Manager with smooth transition between effects.
  • Adaptive particle system for rain and snow.
  • Custom shaders and materials for tailored visual effects.
  • Global volume color control for atmospheric consistency.
  • Particle-based procedural lightning for thunderstorms.
  • Full-screen flash effect for lightning strikes.
  • Mist effect within the snow for a realistic blizzard look.
  • Scalable fog shader for variable environment mood settings.
