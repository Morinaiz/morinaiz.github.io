Pencil Shader Optimization

Upwork Project

Project Overview

For an Upwork client, I developed two versions of an improved pencil shader. The first version strikes a balance between speed and quality, maintaining high visual fidelity while enhancing performance. It's ideal where quality cannot be compromised.

The second version is optimized for speed, making it perfect for mobile devices. While its quality is slightly lower, it's still suitable for mobile applications, offering fast and efficient performance without overburdening the device's resources.
