Weather Effects

Upwork Project

Project Overview

Crafting immersive environments for a 2D cartoon-style game, I have developed three distinct atmospheric effects that simulate dynamic weather patterns and add a touch of realism to gameplay. These effects include a multifaceted rain-thunderstorm scenario, a dense snow-blizzard condition, and a pervasive fog ambience.

Dynamic Rain and Thunderstorm

For the rain effect, I created a system where the length of each rain particle varies with its falling speed, achieving a realistic look and feel for different intensities of a downpour. The thunderstorm is brought to life with custom-made procedural lightnings, striking dramatically within the scene. A custom shader and material work in unison with a global volume for color tuning, ensuring the storm's visual tone is in perfect sync with the game's art style. To complete the effect, a full-screen flash mirrors the dazzling effect of a real lightning strike, adding to the overall storm experience.

Snow Blizzard with Mist

The snow effect mirrors the complexity of its rainy counterpart but is uniquely tailored to represent a blizzard. It eschews lightning and instead incorporates a custom mist effect that resembles fog, creating the illusion of a snowy whiteout. The blending of snow particles and foggy mist conveys the blustery conditions of a blizzard, all while maintaining the game's cartoon aesthetics.

Fog

A tightly optimized full-screen shader achieves the fog effect, overlaying the game with a soft, floating mist. The fog's density and movement are carefully controlled to mimic the ebb and flow of real fog, adding a layer of depth to the game's atmosphere.

Technical Achievements

  • Custom Weather Manager with smooth transition between effects.
  • Adaptive particle system for rain and snow.
  • Custom shaders and materials for tailored visual effects.
  • Global volume color control for atmospheric consistency.
  • Particle-based procedural lightning for thunderstorms.
  • Full-screen flash effect for lightning strikes.
  • Mist effect within the snow for a realistic blizzard look.
  • Scalable fog shader for variable environment mood settings.
